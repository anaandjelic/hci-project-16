How to create a train

You can create a new train by picking a new name and capacity separately for first and second class.

When filling the form you will notice that train will be visualizing. Dark blue circles represent first class seats and lighter blue seats represent second class seats.

Seats will always be numerically marked and first class seats will always be aligned before second class seats.

By pressing the Enter key you will automatically submit the form.

Here are the steps to fill the form successfully:

  1. Enter the train's name. This field can't be empty and can't contain any numerical or special characters.
  2. Enter the first class capacity. This field can only contain digits.
  3. Enter the second class capacity. This field can only contain digits.
  4. By clicking on the Cancel button, you will see a popup notifying you about your intention to discard all of the changes you've made so far. Your form will be cleared only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
  5. By clicking on the Create button or pressing the Enter key, you will see a popup confirming your intention to create a new train using data you just entered. You will create a train only when you press the Yes button in the popup.

How to change a train

You can change a train by selecting the train you want to change from the combobox at the top of the page, entering the new name and first and second class capacity.

When filling the form or choosing a train from the combobox you will notice that the train will be visualizing. Dark blue circles represent first class seats and lighter blue seats represent second class seats.

Seats will always be numerically marked and first class seats will always be aligned before second class seats.

By pressing the Enter key you will automatically submit the form and change the train.

Here are the steps to fill the form successfully:

  1. Select a train from the combobox in the top-left corner
  2. Change the train's name. This field can't be empty and can't contain any numerical or special characters.
  3. Change the first class capacity. This field can only contain digits.
  4. Change the second class capacity. This field can only contain digits.
  5. By clicking on the Delete button, you will see a popup notifying you about your intention to delete delete the train. Train will be deleted only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
  6. By clicking on the Change button or pressing the Enter key, you will see a popup confirming your intention to change the train using data you just entered. You will change the selected train only when you press the Yes button in the popup.

How to create a train line

You can create a new train line by selecting the train you want to traverse this path and by adding, changing and removing stations.

Adding new stations is done by dragging the red pin from the left side of the window to the map. Route will be drawn between the stations when you add more than one of them to the map.

You can check the name of the station on the map by hovering your mouse over them.

Here are the steps to successfully create a new train line.

  1. Pick a train you want to traverse this route from the combobox.
  2. Drag and drop the red pin onto the map to create a new train station.
  3. You will see a popup that lets you enter name, price and time. Name represents the name of the station. Price represents the price of tickets from the first station. Time represents travel time since the first station.
  4. You can remove any of the stations except the first one by double-clicking on the pin representing that station on the map. To be sure about your choice of deletion, hover over the pin to see that station's name.
  5. By clicking on the Cancel button, you will see a popup notifying you about your intention to discard all of the changes you've made so far. Stations and your choice of train will be cleared only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
  6. By clicking on the Create button or pressing the Enter key, you will see a popup confirming your intention to create a new train line using data you just entered. You will create a train line only when you press the Yes button in the popup.

How to create a timetable

You can create a new timetable by selecting the train line from the table on the left side of the screen, selecting days for which you want this timetable to be generated and by selecting time of the departure.

Here are the steps to successfully create a timetable.

  1. Choose the train line you want to create a timetable for. You can do this by clicking on the row that describes that train line.
  2. Choose the days on which you want this train to depart. You can do this by clicking on the desired comboboxes. Marked days will have a blue box next to them, while unmarked days will have a white box next to them.
  3. Choose the time of the departure. You can do this by clicking on the hour you want this train to depart, and then minutes. You can additionally choose if you want the train to depart in the morning or in the afternoon by clicking on the AM or the PM button.
  4. By clicking on the Create button, you will be asked to confirm your intention to create a new timetable. Timetable will be created only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
  5. By clicking on the Cancel button, you will see a popup confirming your intention to discard all of the changes you've made. Changes will be discarded only when you press the Yes button in the popup.

How to change a timetable

You can change a timetable by selecting the timetable configuration from the table on the left side of the screen, selecting days for which you want this timetable to be generated and by selecting time of the departure.

New departures will be generated when you add new days to the configuration.

Departures and tickets wil be modified if time of departure is changed.

Departures and tickets will be deleted on the days you have unchecked.

All changes will be applied only to departures that depart five days after change.

Here are the steps to successfully change a timetable.

  1. Choose the configuration you want to change. You can do this by clicking on the row that describes that configuration.
  2. Choose the days on which you want this train to depart. You can do this by clicking on the desired comboboxes. Marked days will have a blue box next to them, while unmarked days will have a white box next to them.
  3. Choose the time of the departure. You can do this by clicking on the hour you want this train to depart, and then minutes. You can additionally choose if you want the train to depart in the morning or in the afternoon by clicking on the AM or the PM button.
  4. By clicking on the Create button, you will be asked to confirm your intention to create a new timetable. Timetable will be created only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
  5. By clicking on the Delete button, you will see a popup confirming your intention to delete that configuration. Configuration will be deleted only when you press the Yes button in the popup.
