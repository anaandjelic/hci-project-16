How to search a timetable

You can serach a timetable by pressing the "Search" button.

Here are the steps for advanced searching a timetable.

  1. Type in the "from" station this will give you all the lines that contain typed in sation as a starting point.
  2. Type in the "to" station this will give you all the lines that contain typed in sation as an ending point.
  3. Choose the date you can pick the exact day you want to check by clicking on the date picker and simply choosing the desired date.
  4. By clicking on the Search button, you will be shown in the main table the results of your previous requests.

How to search a train line

You can serach train lines by pressing either using the combobox picker or the serach bar.

Here are the steps for successfuly searching train lines with combobox picker.

  1. Simply click on the combobox, after that you will be greeted with all the train stations
  2. Click on the desired one, and it will be shown to you on the map.

Here are the steps for successfuly searching train lines with a search bar.

  1. Type in the search bar the name or names of the desired stations or simply enter the train line id
  2. Press the "Enter" key on the keyboard to get the results in the listbox down below
  3. Select the desired train line, and it will be shown to you on the map
